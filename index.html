Why this matters

Budget once, not every week.

Instead of juggling transfers and spreadsheets, you tell Growspent Budget how to slice each paycheck. We handle the math and the moves.

Money that lands where it should

Decide how much goes to rent, everyday spending, goals, and investing up front. When payday hits, the plan runs automatically.

Room for real life

Update percentages any time things change—new job, new city, or just a tighter month. Your rules adjust without breaking your habit.

Progress you can actually see

Watch goals fill and investments grow right beside your spending, so it’s clear what your paycheck is building for you.
