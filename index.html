Why Growspent?

Most people mean to invest, but life gets in the way. Apps nag, budgets feel restrictive, and money just… gets spent. Growspent quietly redirects a tiny slice of that spending toward your future instead.

The problem
Good intentions, no habit
You know you should invest, but setting up transfers, picking funds, and “being disciplined” is exhausting on top of everything else.
Our idea
Invest from what you already do
Link your spending. Every transaction rounds up or adds a small % that flows into smart buckets: emergency, debt paydown, and long-term investing.
Safety first
Cash-flow aware
Growspent adapts to your balance. If you’re tight on cash, we automatically ease off. When you’re flush, we lean in a bit more.
Multi-bucket
Not just “investing”
Route your growth into three simple buckets: rainy-day savings, paying down expensive debt, and long-term wealth.
Gentle nudge
No guilt, just progress
See small, encouraging updates: “Your coffee runs grew $12.40 this week.” No lectures, no judgment.
Early stage
Help shape v1
Growspent is in prototype phase. Joining now means you can vote on features and get early access when we go live.
